General Guideline

  • UnSexistifyIt is a language game.
  • You earn points as playing the game.
  • You begin at level one with one challenge in hand.
  • You can unlock the new level and new challenge by earning more points.
  • The game is continual and is not against time.
  • You can leave the game by closing your browser tab.
  • Your status and points will store in your browser.

UnSexistifyIt Challenge

INSTRUCTION

  • You are given a sexist statement.
  • Your task is to convert the statement into non-sexist or as less sexist as possible.
  • You must make one change at least.
  • Your modified statement needs to remain meaningful which you may witness in a non-satirical published article or daily news.

HOW TO PLAY

  • Click “Copy Original” button to directly copy the original sentence for easy modification.
  • Click “Skip to Next” button if there is a sentence you don’t understand or find it difficult to modify.
  • Click “Submit” button after modifying, to get the score for your modification and go to the next challenge.

REWARDS

  • There is a predefined base score (100) for every sentence.
  • For each modification you make in the original sentence, a certain amount of points reduce from the base score.
  • The final score you earn for each sentence depends on the number of changes you make.
  • Making changes to certain kind of words incur the more substantial penalty. These words are:
    1. Gender words like: father, wife, woman, boy.
    2. Negative words like: not, don't, aren't, never.
  • Such words are highlighted in the original sentence to remind you that changing them would result in a higher penalty.
  • To earn a high score, make the least number of possible changes.

Rating Challenge

INSTRUCTION

  • You are given a sentence which was modified(unsexistified) by other players in the first challenge.
  • Your task is to rate how sexist the given sentence is.
  • That sentence could be awkward, grammatically incorrect, or meaningless.
  • You can select "Meaningless" option if you think the sentence makes no sense.

HOW TO PLAY

  • Click “Skip to Next” button to skip current sentence and rate the next one.
  • Click “Submit” button after selecting your option, to go to your next challenge.

REWARDS

  • Here the active player does not get points.
  • Instead, the answer is used to calculate how much reward the player gets who created the modified sentence in the “UnSexistifyIt” challenge.
  • The more you think the given sentence is not sexist, the more reward will earn the other player.
  • The other player will lose a massive amount of points if you select the "Meaningless" option.
