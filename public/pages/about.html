ABOUT


    UnSexisitifyIt has been created by Ali Aghelmaleki as part of an academic research project in association with the CSS department of GESIS to collect a corpus of sexist and non-sexist data generated via crowdsourcing. This game has been motivated by unfun.me
    As you play this game, you are contributing valuable data that may help us get a little bit closer to our potential goals, including:
  • Create a probabilistic classifier for single sentences into sexist/non-sexist or even additionally sexist against men or women.
  • Understand how heterogeneous human opinions about sexism are, otherwise, what is the ground truth for this classifier?
  • Understand the impact of content features, e.g. simple word choice, semantics, etc. against context features including said by whom, speaker's gender and reputation and in what medium for judging something sexist.
  • Is there a difference in the perception of sexism against women and sexism against men?
